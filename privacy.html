Privacy — the short version

Tax Tjommie stores your data locally on your device. It never leaves your browser unless you explicitly export it.

The one exception: AI categorisation

When you upload a bank statement, we send a sanitised version of each transaction — only date, description, and amount — to Anthropic (Claude) for categorisation. Before sending, we strip:

  • Account numbers and card numbers
  • Reference numbers longer than 10 digits
  • Running balances
  • Any other field not on the allow-list

Your account numbers never leave your device.

No analytics, no tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracker. We do not sell or share data.

Your data, your control

Settings → Data lets you export your data as JSON, import it on a new device, or wipe everything.
